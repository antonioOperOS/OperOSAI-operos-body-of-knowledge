# Sales Enablement

## Governing principle: trust-first, not trial-first

AI buyers in 2025-2026 are skeptical, not curious — they've been burned by tools that demoed well and broke in production. OperOS sales motion is built around this explicitly:

- Lead with the specific production/compliance failure the buyer has already experienced — not a generic demo.
- Show real results on the client's own data/use case wherever possible, not a canned walkthrough.
- Skip the self-serve free trial in favor of a scoped, paid pilot (Sprint 0) with a defined success criteria before commitment.
- Provide human support from day one.
- Every pitch must translate its result into RoT terms (revenue per token, vs. holdout) — see `core-concepts/RoT-return-on-tokens.md`. No sales asset is Published without this.

## Real, confirmed offer (do not invent alternatives)

- **Sprint 0** (the actual first offer): $15,390/mo fixed for 6 weeks, plus a variable take-rate on the client's token/compute consumption (cost + 20% markup proposed, formula not finalized).
- **Post-pilot tiers:** Starter $3,500/mo (<=25 users), Growth $6,500/mo (<=75 users), Enterprise $12,000/mo+ (SOC 2, dedicated CS).
