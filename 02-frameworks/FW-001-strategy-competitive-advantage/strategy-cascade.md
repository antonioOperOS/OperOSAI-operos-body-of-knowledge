# Strategy Cascade

## Purpose

Translate market reality into coherent choices, capability bets, operating cadence, and measurable execution.

## Visual model

```text
Market Reality
  -> Strategic Diagnosis
    -> Guiding Choices
      -> Capability Bets
        -> Operating Cadence
          -> AI-Enabled Execution
            -> Metrics and Learning
```

## Inputs

- Market and customer signals
- Customer pain, urgency, and willingness to pay
- Competitive landscape
- Internal capabilities and constraints
- Current strategic initiatives
- Financial model and resource capacity
- AI readiness and knowledge maturity

## Steps

1. Diagnose the market reality.
2. Name the core strategic problem.
3. Define guiding choices and explicit tradeoffs.
4. Select capability bets that create advantage.
5. Convert choices into operating cadence.
6. Identify AI-enabled workflows that accelerate execution.
7. Instrument leading and lagging metrics.
8. Review learning loops monthly.

## Outputs

- One-page strategic diagnosis
- Guiding policy statement
- Strategic tradeoff map
- Capability bet portfolio
- AI leverage plan
- Strategy KPI dashboard
- Monthly strategy review agenda

## Metrics

- Strategic focus score
- Initiative-to-outcome alignment
- Resource concentration ratio
- Decision-to-action cycle time
- AI-enabled workflow adoption
- Customer outcome movement
- Strategic learning velocity

## Failure modes

- Vague aspirations without tradeoffs
- Too many priorities
- Initiatives disconnected from capabilities
- AI projects not linked to strategic outcomes
- KPIs measuring activity instead of advantage
- No operating cadence to reinforce strategy

## AI augmentation

OperOS can ingest customer, market, CRM, product, finance, and operating data to identify strategic tensions, draft diagnosis briefs, detect initiative sprawl, map work to OKRs, and recommend executive review topics.

## OperOS implementation path

1. Configure strategic object types in the knowledge graph.
2. Connect planning documents, KPI dashboards, and decision logs.
3. Run a strategy diagnosis workshop.
4. Map existing initiatives to outcomes and capabilities.
5. Create the Strategy Cascade workspace.
6. Deploy AI strategy brief generation.
7. Add monthly strategic review automation.
